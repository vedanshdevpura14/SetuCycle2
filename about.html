About Setu-Cycle

Our Mission for a Sustainable Future

Our Story

Setu-Cycle was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable ecosystem for waste management that benefits both individuals and the environment. Our journey began when we witnessed the alarming levels of waste pollution in urban areas and recognized the urgent need for an innovative solution.

Today, Setu-Cycle has grown into a comprehensive recycling platform that connects households, businesses, and waste collectors, creating a circular economy that transforms waste into valuable resources.

Our Mission

At Setu-Cycle, our mission is to revolutionize waste management by making recycling convenient, rewarding, and accessible to everyone. We aim to reduce landfill waste, promote sustainable practices, and create a cleaner environment for future generations.

Through our innovative platform, we empower individuals to become active participants in the recycling movement while providing waste collectors with better opportunities and resources.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices that minimize waste and conserve resources.

Community

We believe in the power of collective action and building strong communities for positive change.

Innovation

We continuously seek creative solutions to improve waste management and recycling processes.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practices in all aspects of our business.
